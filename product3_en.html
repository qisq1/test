reat Xinjiang is a vast and beautiful place. In this hot land, once conceived a brilliant civilization. Today, in the 21st century, the Belt and Road has given her more vitality. Xinjiang is one of the main production areas of cashmere in China, and the cashmere industry will receive unprecedented development. Compared with cashmere in Inner Mongolia and other places, cashmere fibers in Xinjiang are more delicate and shorter, and cashmere fibers in Inner Mongolia and other places are thicker and longer, but the thermal effect is similar. The cashmere sweater, cashmere quilt, cashmere shawl and other products produced by Xinjiang cashmere have the characteristics of light, thin and soft, and are relatively more comfortable. 

Xinjiang Dingkun Textile Co., Ltd. was founded in 2016. It is a large group company with cashmere industry, which integrates brand marketing, clothing design, industrial consulting, clothing fabrics printing and dyeing. Dingkun has an independent industrial park and covers an area of 40,000 square meters. This is a key industrial park featuring garment production, processing and export in the Greater Xinjiang region. And it was awarded as a demonstration base of Xinjiang Garment and Textile Industry and It was listed as a manufacturing base by the Xinjiang Economic Commission.
